# 🗺️ ONBP Roadmap

> **Purpose:** Define the long-term development plan, milestones, and future vision for the Open Node Backend Platform (ONBP).

---

# 🎯 Purpose

The ONBP Roadmap provides a structured development plan for the platform.

It serves as a guide for contributors, maintainers, and users by outlining upcoming milestones, priorities, and long-term goals.

The roadmap is a living document and will evolve as ONBP grows.

---

# 🌍 Vision

The long-term vision of ONBP is to become a modern, open-source backend platform that enables developers to build scalable, secure, and maintainable business applications with minimal setup.

The platform should support projects such as:

- Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP)
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
- Inventory Management
- Manufacturing Systems
- Transport Management
- Human Resource Management
- Hospital Management
- School & College Management
- E-Commerce Platforms
- Government Applications
- Custom Enterprise Solutions

---

# 🚀 Development Principles

The roadmap follows these principles:

- Build a strong foundation first.
- Deliver working software in every milestone.
- Keep the platform modular.
- Prioritize quality over speed.
- Remain open source.
- Prefer free technologies.
- Maintain backward compatibility where practical.
